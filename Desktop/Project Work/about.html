About Room Finder

Room Finder is a trusted platform designed to help individuals, students, and families easily find rooms, flats, or houses for rent across Nepal. Whether you're relocating for work, study, or a fresh start—our platform simplifies your search.

We provide verified listings, complete with photos and essential details, so you can browse confidently and make informed decisions before visiting the place.

Why Choose Us?

  • Simple, user-friendly interface
  • Search by location, price, or room type
  • Verified listings with direct contact options
  • No middlemen or hidden charges
  • Updated frequently with new listings

Our Mission

We aim to make room-hunting quick, trustworthy, and convenient. By connecting landlords and tenants directly, we reduce effort and stress—so you can focus on starting the next chapter of your life.
